1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us when you create an account, make a booking, or contact us. This may include:

  • Your full name, email address, and phone number
  • Appointment preferences, service history, and stylist preferences
  • Payment method details (processed securely via our payment provider — we do not store card numbers)
  • Messages, feedback, and reviews you submit to us

We also collect limited technical data automatically, including your IP address, browser type, and pages visited, to maintain the security and performance of our website.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Process and confirm your bookings and appointments
  • Send appointment reminders and follow-up messages via SMS and email
  • Improve the quality and personalisation of our services
  • Send promotional offers and newsletters — only if you have opted in
  • Respond to your enquiries and support requests
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. SMS and Email Communications

By providing your phone number and email address, you consent to receive transactional messages related to your appointments — including confirmations, reminders, and cancellation notices.

Promotional communications (offers, loyalty rewards, new services) are only sent if you have explicitly opted in. You may opt out at any time by updating your account preferences or by replying STOP to any SMS.

4. Data Security

We take the security of your personal data seriously. Our security measures include:

  • Encrypted password storage using industry-standard hashing
  • HTTPS encryption for all data transmitted via our website
  • Restricted staff access to personal data on a need-to-know basis
  • Regular security reviews of our systems and third-party services

While we take every reasonable precaution, no system is completely secure. If you believe your account has been compromised, please contact us immediately.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information to any third party. We may share your data only with trusted service providers who help us operate our business — such as SMS delivery services, email providers, and payment processors — and only to the extent necessary to deliver our services.

These providers are contractually bound to handle your data securely and in accordance with applicable privacy laws.

6. Cookies

Our website uses cookies to maintain your session, remember your preferences, and analyse website performance. We use:

  • Strictly necessary cookies — required for the website to function (e.g. login sessions, CSRF protection)
  • Analytics cookies — to understand how visitors use our site so we can improve it (e.g. Google Analytics with anonymised IP)

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling certain cookies may affect the functionality of our website.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct any inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Delete your account and associated personal data
  • Withdraw consent for marketing communications at any time
  • Object to or restrict certain uses of your data

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the details below or through your account settings.
